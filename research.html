Research Interests

I was introduced to the world of Machine Learning when I did an internship in a startup Likewyss.I started to love ML and decided to do more research on this area.I was so inspired by motto "Goodbye Irrelevance,See News that matters" which is the sole motto of Likewyss.So as a part of my undergraduate project I developed a web portal which creates a personalized dashboard for the user by streaming web pages suiting user's interests using a semi-supervised approach by combining both document classification and document clustering techniques.
